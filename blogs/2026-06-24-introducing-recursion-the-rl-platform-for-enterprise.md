Labelbox introduced Recursion, a reinforcement learning platform that specializes AI agents for specific enterprise business workflows. A Recursion-tuned 35B open-source model outperformed a 397B general model by 85% on financial tasks, while a customer service agent reached an 84% resolution rate with a 72% reduction in hallucinations at $32 per 1,000 tickets versus $158-$176 for competing solutions.
